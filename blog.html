  • LET YOUR CONFESSIONS REFLECT IN YOUR LIFESTYLE!

    In my little years in the faith, there is something i have noticed among some of us who are believers, especially with respect to those who know 'who they are in Christ', and this is what i want to briefly point out.

    I have seen some believers who are masters of confessions. I mean when you see them talking you will be amazed. For example, you'll hear, 'Yess, Gloraaaay, I'm the righteousness of God in Christ Jesus, I'm holy, blameless, faultless, I'm loved of the father, there is no sin in me, i am what the word says i am, i live and reign in life, etc. Now, these words are very very true. They are what we ALL have in redemption.

    But how come some of us who confess these, live the opposite of what we confess? Why are we full of just words, yet lacking in actions that corresponds with our confessions? Read more
