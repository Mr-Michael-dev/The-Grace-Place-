About Us

The Grace Place was founded in 2019 by Victor Ehwarieme (lead pastor) out of a burning desire to see men come into the reality of their true nature in Christ and not being tossed to and fro by every wind of doctrine.

Vision

 To Rasie Men who shall help men to see God in men by the instrumentality of the preaching of the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Mission

 Unveiling the whole counsel of God as revealed in Christ Jesus, and building upon the foundation of the early Apostles (Witnesses).

Mandate

 We are sent to testify of Him (Christ) John 20 vs 21, Matthew 28 vs 18 - 19, Mark 16 vs 15 - 19.
